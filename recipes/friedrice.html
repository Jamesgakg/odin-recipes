Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a rice dish originating from China, is it commonly served as a way to use leftover rice and whatever other ingredients are lying around consisting of aromatics, vegetables and protien all fried in a wok.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • Sesame oil
  • fresh Garlic
  • fresh Chilli
  • 2 eggs
  • cooked Rice
  • frozen Green peas
  • soy sauce
  • baby carrots

Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a rice dish originating from China, is it commonly served as a way to use leftover rice and whatever other ingredients are lying around consisting of aromatics, vegetables and protien all fried in a wok.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • Sesame oil
  • fresh Garlic
  • fresh Chilli
  • 2 eggs
  • cooked Rice
  • frozen Green peas
  • soy sauce
  • baby carrots
  • 300g Chicken Thighs

Steps

  1. Thinly slice fresh Chilli
  2. Grate, thinly slice or mince (based on personal preference) 2 large cloves of Garlic
  3. Weigh out 2 cups of cooked Rice (it is best to use 1 or 2 day old cooked rice because it has "dried" out and will absorb the flavours)
  4. Slice up baby carrots
  5. Weigh out 1/2 cup of frozen Green Peas
  6. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl, add some salt and wisk them
  7. Dice up chicken thighs into small bite size pieces
  8. Weigh up 2 separate servings of 10g of Sesame oil
  9. Weigh 15g of soy sauce
  10. Grab a Wok, place it on the stove and turn the heat up to the highest setting
  11. Its very important here to be fully prepped with ingredients, utensils and equipment before proceeding as this dish goes together very quickly
  12. As soon as the wok starts to smoke a little, add 1 of the 10g servings of Sesame oil into the wok
  13. As soon as the oil starts to smoke, add the aromatics (chilli and garlic) and cook them for 15-25 seconds to release the flavour
  14. Throw in the diced up Chicken Thighs, lightly season with salt then mix with the aromatics using a stainless steel wok spatula
  15. Cook the Chicken Thighs untill its fully cooked through (the smaller the chicken has been diced, the quicker it will cook)
  16. Remove cooked Chicken Thighs from the wok while leaving the excess fat/oil in the wok and place cooked chicken into a small bowl and set aside
  17. Let the excess fat/oil in the wok heat up and smoke again
  18. Add sliced baby carrots, add frozen Green peas then season with salt
  19. Saute' baby carrots and Green peas until they are semi-cooked
  20. Remove baby carrots and Green peas from wok and place into a small bowl
  21. Add the 2nd 10g serving of Sesame oil into the wok, let the wok heat up again until the oil starts to smoke
  22. Pour the eggs into the wok then immediately throw the cooked Rice into the wok
  23. Mix, stir the eggs and rice together thoroughly using a stainless steel wok spatula
  24. Add the cooked Chicken thighs into the wok, add the saute'd baby carrots and Green peas into the wok
  25. Add the 15g serving of soy sauce into the wok then stir and mix it all together
  26. Turn heat off, continue stiring
  27. Finally use a teaspoon to taste the dish and sparingly add aditional soy sauce then stir untill desired flavour is achived
  28. Serve and Enjoy!

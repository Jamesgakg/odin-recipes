Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish originating from Rome, typically consisting of spaghetti (or some variant) mixed with pork, cheese, eggs and black pepper.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 200g Spaghetti or Spagettoni
  • 3 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 100g Guanciale (cured pork cheek)
  • fresh ground black pepper
